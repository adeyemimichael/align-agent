This document explains each menu section in the Align AI Productivity Agent, what it does, and why it's crucial for winning the hackathon.

## 2. ‚úÖ Daily Check-in
**What it does:**
- Interactive form with sliders for Energy (1-10), Sleep Quality (1-10), Stress Level (1-10)
- Mood selector with visual icons (Positive/Neutral/Negative)
- Calculates capacity score using AI-powered algorithm
- Determines optimal mode (Recovery/Balanced/Deep Work) based on your inputs
- Stores historical data for pattern learning

**Why it's important for the hackathon:**
- **Human-Centered Design**: Acknowledges that productivity isn't constant - it varies based on how you feel
- **Scientific Approach**: Uses multiple factors (energy, sleep, stress, mood) like real productivity research
- **Beautiful UX**: Color-coded sliders with gradient badges make it engaging, not a chore
- **AI Foundation**: This data feeds the AI to make intelligent planning decisions
- **Differentiation**: Most productivity apps ignore your actual state - we make it central

**Hackathon Pitch Points:**
- "Traditional apps assume you have the same capacity every day - we know that's not realistic"
- "Our check-in takes 30 seconds but gives the AI crucial context for your entire day"
- "The capacity score isn't arbitrary - it's calculated from real psychological factors"
- "This is the foundation of our adaptive AI - garbage in, garbage out, so we make input delightful"

## Overall Hackathon Strategy

### Why This Menu Structure Wins:

1. **Complete Product**: Not a prototype - a fully functional app with all expected features
2. **AI Throughout**: Every section either uses AI or feeds data to AI
3. **Beautiful Design**: Professional UI that looks like a real product
4. **Real Integrations**: Works with Todoist, Google Calendar - not isolated
5. **Human-Centered**: Acknowledges that humans aren't robots - capacity varies
6. **Data-Driven**: Analytics and patterns show we're serious about productivity science
7. **Transparent**: Shows AI reasoning, explains data usage
8. **Polished**: Working toggles, smooth animations, consistent design language

### Demo Flow for Judges:

1. **Start at Dashboard** - "This is Align, an AI productivity agent that adapts to YOUR capacity"
2. **Show Check-in** - "Unlike other apps, we start by understanding how you're feeling today"
3. **Generate Plan** - "Watch the AI create a realistic schedule based on your capacity"
4. **Show AI Reasoning** - "See? It's not a black box - you understand the decisions"
5. **Show Integrations** - "We work with tools you already use - Todoist, Google Calendar"
6. **Show Analytics** - "Over time, we learn your patterns and get smarter"
7. **Show Goals** - "And it's all aligned with your long-term goals, not just busy work"

### Key Differentiators:

- **Adaptive AI**: Plans change based on YOUR capacity, not generic rules
- **Human-Centered**: Acknowledges you're not a robot
- **Transparent**: Shows AI reasoning
- **Integrated**: Works with existing tools
- **Beautiful**: Professional design that impresses
- **Complete**: Every feature works, nothing is "coming soon" (except notifications)

### Winning Pitch:

"Most productivity apps treat you like a robot - they assume you have the same capacity every day. Align is different. We use AI to adapt to YOUR actual capacity. Start your day with a 30-second check-in. Our AI analyzes your energy, sleep, stress, and mood to calculate your capacity score. Then it generates a realistic daily plan - not an overwhelming list, but a schedule you can actually complete. We integrate with Todoist and Google Calendar, so you don't abandon your existing workflow. And we learn over time - our analytics detect your patterns and the AI gets smarter. This isn't just another todo app. It's an AI agent that understands you're human."
